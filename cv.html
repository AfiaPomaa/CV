Open and Transparent Dialogue

My most recent experience as a residence assistance required open and transparent dialogue at every stage. I was the first point of contact for 44 students, I was the support for the students on your floor. In order to efficiently support students, I needed to create a relationship with them from the get go, so that they would feel comfortbale coming to me in times of crisis. I did this by doing a door to door snack delivery in the first week to introduce myself. I was also responsible for organizing weekly events, creating posters, liaison with my students, managers and co-workers. This required transparent dialogue with my team both during planning and the execution of events.

Developing People Through Empowerment with Accountability and Performance Management

I developed people through empowerment by keeping up with weekly check-ins. I managed their performance by keeping a record of their mental and academic status as was reported during the weekly meetings. With this technique, I was able to keep them accountable by reminding them of the goals they set for themselves and shared with me in the beginning of the semester.
